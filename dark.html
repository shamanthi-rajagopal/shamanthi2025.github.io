About

About

Is the library your usual study spot? Is it the place that gets you in the ZONE? Can't go to the library right now, but need to study urgently? Welcome to 'My Focus Library'! This virtual library experience enables you to bring the library to you. Sit back, relax, wherever you are, and start studying. Good luck!

Inspired by: 'I Miss My Cafe,' 'I Miss My Library,' and 'I Miss My Bar.' Audio accessed from 'freesound.org'. No Copy Right Infringement Intended.

Coded & Illustrated by Shamanthi Rajagopal.



How to Use Timer: (25 for Pomodoro)

Enter the desired time. Press the START button. Study while time runs. Press the RESET button to reset timer. Don't press TOO many times...be patient. Reload page if any issues.
